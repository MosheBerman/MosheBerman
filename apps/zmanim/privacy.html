1. Preamble

I take your privacy very seriously. Read on to see what sensitive features Ultimate Zmanim uses, and how.

2. Privacy Sensitive Features in Ultimate Zmanim

  • Location

    Ultimate Zmanim uses your location to calculate sunrise and sunset in your precise location. This is how we generate the zmanim data provided to you in the app.

    I do not send your location to any servers or share it with anyone. It does not leave your device, with one exception. Google Analytics shows me approximately where my users are.
